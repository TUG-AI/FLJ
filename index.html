Join Us for a Book Study

We're reading Fight Like Jesus by Jason Porterfield this Lent and would love to have you join us! Each week we'll explore how Jesus waged peace during Holy Week.

The readings are short (~20 pages per week) and the conversation is always good. Come as you are!
